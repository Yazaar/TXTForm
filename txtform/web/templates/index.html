This is TXTForm

Link your accounts and gain access to their information through simple to use API requests.

Let your Twitch stream know what is currently playing on Spotify by linking your Spotify account!

You in control

Fully customizable responses

Add components such as free text and Spotify specific information in your responses.

Decide response depending on scenario

Have one response for when the stream is offline, another for when the stream is online!

First state from the top is returned as the response, here response named playing is returned if live else offline.
